## Project owner

Merryfair (merryfair.com) — Malaysian ergonomic office chair manufacturer, established 1974. Blog lives at `https://www.merryfair.com/latest_updates/blog/[slug]/`. 31 blog posts currently published. The blog serves both SEO acquisition and brand/EEAT authority purposes.

## Strategic foundation

All decisions follow the principles in `seo-strategy-context.md` (original strategy document from a prior Claude chat). Key principles that have been explicitly confirmed by the project owner:

- **Never noindex or delete existing posts without checking ALL value dimensions.** A post with zero organic traffic can still be valuable for topical authority, EEAT, conversion contribution, internal link centrality, or cluster completeness. The owner explicitly corrected an early triage that flagged 8 zero-traffic posts as noindex candidates — all were retained.
- **Search volume alone is not a proxy for content value.** Value is multi-dimensional.
- **The map (merryfair_content_map.json) is the source of truth.** Every decision should be made with the map data.
- **The AI is a thinking partner, not an oracle.** Every suggestion must be interrogated against business knowledge the data can't capture.
- **Structure serves topical authority, not the other way around.** Pillar pages and hub-and-spoke are implementation patterns toward comprehensive topic coverage, not goals in themselves.
